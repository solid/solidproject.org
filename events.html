Solid Events provide an opportunity to meet others who are working on Solid or are interested in Solid. Solid Events are run by people like you. If you are interested in coordinating a Solid Event near you, reach out to solid+events@theodi.org.

  • Interested in speaking at an upcoming Solid World? Send an email to solid+world@theodi.org.
  • Have another Solid oriented event to share? Send an email to solid+events@theodi.org.
