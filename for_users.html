For users

Your data follows you, not the app

You decide which apps and AI agents can touch your Pod, and what they see when they do. Switch apps and your information stays with you.

Get a Pod Browse the apps

Unlike many of today's platforms, Solid returns the web to its original vision of empowering individuals and enabling collaboration.

You choose which applications to use with your data rather than being locked into specific platforms. New services, applications and AI agents can access your information instantly with your permission, encouraging innovation. Your data remains yours even when you try new services, applications or AI agents.
