Solid For Organizations

Solid was originally developed by Sir Tim Berners-Lee's team at MIT in collaboration with the Qatar Computing Research Institute and Oxford University and has been nurtured by a dedicated global community for many years.

The organizations listed below have worked in a range of capacities to support the deployment and research of Solid technology.
