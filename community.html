Solid is powered by a community of passionate volunteers committed to the project's success.

As of October 2024, the Open Data Institute (ODI) has committed to provide organisational stewardship and support to the project.

Everyone is welcome in the Solid community - discover ways to join and contribute to the Solid community.

Where to Collaborate

  • Solid Project Forum

    Create and respond to comments, questions, and discussions happening in the community.

  • Solid Matrix Chat

    The Solid Project room on Matrix is a live chat where community members can connect on everything Solid related.

  • W3C Solid Community Group

    Learn about and work on Solid specifications.

Meet the community

The Solid Events page lists online and in person meetings relevant to the Solid ecosystem.
